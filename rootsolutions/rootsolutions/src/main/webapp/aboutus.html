We are desining an application in which people have to register initially , and their health conditions like blood pressure,diabetes,pulse rate we will be recorded in a database.Every month the database will be recorded i a database. Every month the database will be updated and a graph will be generated based on the data. If their health condition are in normal range then it will generate a normal graph. If their health conditions are beyond the normal range then it will show a critical graph/condition. With this application it is benefical for patients who are suffering to connect with their physicians and follow up their prescribed medication. This application is mainly for people in ruural villages where their health conditions will be continiously mentioned and will be informed at the earliest.
